5. Marketing & Customer Acquisition

Key Principle

This is a high-automation, low-overhead business. One person (the founder) plus AI handles marketing, content, and support. No team needed until $50K MRR. The hybrid model below maximizes reach while keeping CAC near zero for the majority of users.

AI-Automated Channels (Low Overhead)

Viral Score Cards

AI generates shareable "What Judges Saw" score breakdown images for social media. Each share is a free ad targeting the exact right audience.

$0 CAC • High volume • Built-in viral loop

Email Drip Sequences

Automated onboarding, upgrade nudges at 3-analysis cap, re-engagement at 14-day inactivity. Lifecycle emails drive conversion without manual effort.

$0 CAC • Retention + conversion driver

SEO Content Engine

AI-generated articles: deduction breakdowns, scoring guides, "What is a Level 5 floor routine?" content. Captures organic search traffic from parents researching gymnastics scoring.

$0 CAC • Compounds over time • High-intent traffic

In-App Referral Program

"Share with a gym friend — both get a free analysis." Peer referrals carry maximum trust in the gymnastics parent community.

$2–5 CAC • High volume • 28–70x LTV:CAC

Automated Coach Outreach

Templated email campaigns to USAG-registered coaches with personalized video demos. One coach acquisition = 20–60 athlete subscriptions downstream.

$15–25/coach • Effective $0.50–1.25/user • 112–280x LTV:CAC

Personal Outreach (Targeted B2B)

Competition Presence

Attend 5–10 regional meets per season. Live demos at the scoring table. Parents see results in real-time while their child competes.

$500–2K/meet • 50–100 signups/meet • $10–20 CAC

Gym Owner Meetings

Personal pitch to 20–30 top competitive gyms. $149/mo site license deals. Each gym = captive audience of 50–200 competitive athletes.

$0 CAC (time only) • $1,788 ARR per deal

USAG Congress Presentations

Apply to speak at regional USAG congresses. Free attendance, high credibility with coaches and gym owners. Position as thought leader in gymnastics technology.

Free • High credibility • Coach pipeline

Gymnastics Influencer Partnerships

Partner with 10 nano-influencers (5K–100K followers). Free accounts + commission on referrals. Authentic endorsements from athletes and coaches the community trusts.

8. AI Development Roadmap

The Data Moat

Every analysis Strive performs is a labeled training data point: video in, structured scoring judgments out. By 100K analyses, Strive will own the most valuable gymnastics scoring dataset in existence. No competitor can replicate this without years of data collection and the same user base. This is the most defensible asset in the business.

NOW API-First (Ship Fast) 0–50K analyses

Engine: Gemini 2.5 Flash • Cost: $0.018/analysis

  • 2-pass analysis: Detect skills → Judge with USAG calibration
  • Seed parameter for scoring consistency (±0.10 tolerance)
  • Claude Sonnet 4 fallback for redundancy
  • Focus: Ship product, acquire users, collect training data
50K Begin Fine-Tuning ~Month 9–12

Investment: ~$5K training cost

  • Fine-tune open-source vision model on 50K labeled analysis pairs
  • Train on Strive's proprietary scoring data (video → structured deductions)
  • A/B test fine-tuned model vs. Gemini on accuracy and speed
  • Goal: Match Gemini accuracy at 1/3 the cost
100K Hybrid Model ~Month 12–18

Cost reduction: $0.018 → ~$0.005/analysis

  • Own model handles 70% of analyses (straightforward routines)
  • Gemini fallback for complex or edge-case routines
  • Confidence scoring: route to Gemini when own model is uncertain
  • Margin impact: Gross margin increases from 85% to ~93%
250K+ Self-Hosted Dominance Month 18+

Cost: ~$0.002/analysis

  • Mostly self-hosted model, Gemini only for edge cases (<10%)
  • Real-time analysis possible (sub-5 second response)
  • Multi-sport models trained on shared architecture
  • Competitive moat: Proprietary model + 250K labeled dataset = years of head start over any new entrant

10. UX & Product Strategy

Progressive Data Disclosure

Three-layer information architecture ensures every user tier gets value while creating natural upgrade motivation:

Layer 1: Free Teaser

Overall score, 2-sentence summary, top deduction. Enough to prove value, not enough to satisfy.

Layer 2: Competitive Full

Complete skill breakdown, all deductions with severity, biomechanics angles, drill recommendations.

Layer 3: Elite Expert

What-if simulator, D-score analysis, coach sharing, recruiting portfolio, competition prep tools.

Persona-Branched Onboarding

Parent Flow

3 steps

Name, child's level, first video upload. Minimal friction, immediate value.

Athlete Flow

7 steps

Profile, level, events, goals, training schedule, first analysis, progress baseline.

Coach Flow

Roster setup

Gym info, athlete roster import, team creation, bulk analysis walkthrough.

Emotional Design

  • Score reveal animation: Dramatic unveil moment that mirrors the anticipation of waiting for scores at a meet
  • Improvement celebration: Confetti and milestone animations when scores improve over time
  • Graceful bad-score handling: Encouraging language, focus on growth areas, never punitive framing
  • Daily encouragement: Dashboard displays motivational insights across all tiers

Retention Architecture

Score Path Visualization

Historical score graph showing improvement trajectory. Becomes the most valuable asset in the app — the reason users cannot leave.

Achievement Milestones

Badge system for consistency (streak), improvement (new personal bests), and mastery (skill completion).

Weekly Digest

Email/push summary of week's analyses, progress vs. previous week, upcoming meet reminders.

Training Tab

Dedicated tab with personalized drills, nutrition tips, mental preparation, and strength training — keeps app open between analyses.
