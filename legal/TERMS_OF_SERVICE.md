## 2. Description of Service

Strive is an AI-powered gymnastics video analysis platform. Users upload videos of gymnastic routines, and Strive's analysis engine provides:

- **Estimated scores** based on USAG-style deduction frameworks
- **Deduction breakdowns** identifying specific faults in execution, form, and technique
- **Training suggestions** including drill recommendations and conditioning guidance
- **Injury awareness flags** highlighting skills that carry elevated physical risk
- **Progress tracking** across multiple sessions and routines

Strive is designed as a **supplemental training tool**. It is **not** a replacement for qualified coaching, professional instruction, or medical advice.
