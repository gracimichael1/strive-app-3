8. Legal & Compliance

6 launch blockers identified. Non-negotiable before public release.

COPPA Compliance

Platform targets minors under 13. Full COPPA compliance with verifiable parental consent required.

Deadline: April 22, 2026 (1 month away)

Requires: age gate, parental consent flow, data minimization, deletion rights, privacy policy updates.

Illinois BIPA Exposure

MediaPipe pose detection on minors creates biometric data liability. Illinois BIPA carries $1,000–$5,000 per violation statutory damages.

Mitigation: Explicit biometric consent, data retention limits, consider server-side-only processing.

Privacy Policy & ToS

Neither document exists. Required before any public launch. Must cover: data collection, AI analysis, video storage, third-party sharing, deletion rights.

Business Entity Formation

Delaware C-Corp recommended for investor compatibility. Needed for: Stripe merchant account, liability protection, IP assignment, future fundraising.

Misleading Privacy Claim

UI previously stated “on-device processing” while sending video to Google servers. FTC Section 5 deceptive practices violation. FIXED

Insurance Requirements

Tech E&O + Cyber liability insurance: ~$2–5K/year. Required before processing user data at scale.

Additional Legal Considerations

Trademark Risk

“Strive” has existing conflicts in the fitness space. Trademark search and potential rebranding should be evaluated before major marketing spend.

SafeSport Compliance

If partnering with USA Gymnastics or USAG-affiliated organizations, SafeSport compliance policies and training may be required.

9. UX & Product Strategy

Progressive Data Disclosure

Three-layer information architecture ensures every user tier gets value while creating natural upgrade motivation:

Layer 1: Free Teaser

Overall score, 2-sentence summary, top deduction. Enough to prove value, not enough to satisfy.

Layer 2: Competitive Full

Complete skill breakdown, all deductions with severity, biomechanics angles, drill recommendations.

Layer 3: Elite Expert

What-if simulator, D-score analysis, coach sharing, recruiting portfolio, competition prep tools.

Persona-Branched Onboarding

Parent Flow

3 steps

Name, child's level, first video upload. Minimal friction, immediate value.

Athlete Flow

7 steps

Profile, level, events, goals, training schedule, first analysis, progress baseline.

Coach Flow

Roster setup

Gym info, athlete roster import, team creation, bulk analysis walkthrough.

Emotional Design

  • Score reveal animation: Dramatic unveil moment that mirrors the anticipation of waiting for scores at a meet
  • Improvement celebration: Confetti and milestone animations when scores improve over time
  • Graceful bad-score handling: Encouraging language, focus on growth areas, never punitive framing
  • Daily encouragement: Dashboard displays motivational insights across all tiers

Retention Architecture

Score Path Visualization

Historical score graph showing improvement trajectory. Becomes the most valuable asset in the app — the reason users cannot leave.

Achievement Milestones

Badge system for consistency (streak), improvement (new personal bests), and mastery (skill completion).

Weekly Digest

Email/push summary of week's analyses, progress vs. previous week, upcoming meet reminders.

Training Tab

Dedicated tab with personalized drills, nutrition tips, mental preparation, and strength training — keeps app open between analyses.
